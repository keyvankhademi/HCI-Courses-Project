What We Teach

A Site to Host and Analyze University Course Syllabi

This is an online tool to gather, host, and analyze educational patterns in university course syllabi. Using the syllabus information, we wish to gain a better understanding of topic coverage, theories taught, and techniques practiced in the classroom. Furthermore, this tool enables regional comparisons of the data in the system. Ultimately, we hope to uncover pedagogical choices made in our courses by analyzing syllabi information.
